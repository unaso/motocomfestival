HOW TO GET THERE

2018/9/29(土）Event time 10:00~22:00

Tokyo Sumida-ku Yokoami 1-3-20


Tomorrow's NEUTRAL event is ON! Rain or shine, we're gonna have a blast!

In case of rain, we will have tents. You will be able to enjoy great riders content, art, music, food & drinks at the venue from 10 am.

Our artists Adrian Hogan and Hama-House Illustrations will paint a Vespa Primavera 150 provided by Vespa Japan. We will have Kelly Ann Nguyen & Emily O'Marra paint helmets, while Takahashi-san aka DJ Yonekura spins the music. You will be able to take the newest Japanese motorcycles for a test ride, and also try out グラフィット / glafit e-bikes. Those arriving by bike will get a special surprise, and everyone taking public transportation will be able to enjoy Baird Beer - craft beer. There will be custom bikes, food trucks and NEUTRAL apparel, as well as 株式会社kadoya Leathers of course! Professional photos will be taken by Blue Lion Tokyo/ Michael Holmes Photo.

See you there!
