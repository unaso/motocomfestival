Nothing matches the feeling of freedom like riding a motorcycle.

The street, the wind, the beautiful landscapes put all worries of the real world behind and let you focus on what's most important: The beauty of the present moment and your individuality in relation to the world. At the NEUTRAL event, we will share this feeling with a crowd during a Ryogoku street party. In addition to exhibition of custom bikes, part and gear sales and showcase of latest, as well as some vintage bike models, our NEUTRAL artists will create motorcycle-inspired artwork. We will witness live painting of motorcycle parts by selected artists, and parts painted at this event will be assembled into a new motorcycle at the next, follow-up neutral event.


Motorcycles are an assurance of a sensational lifestyle. Experience it in Ryogoku.
